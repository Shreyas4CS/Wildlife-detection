Teach by sound

🔊 Train animals by their call

mic off

Turn on the microphone, play or make the animal's sound, then hold Record for 7 seconds. The system averages the sound's frequency fingerprint over that window — pair it with the same name you used for its picture.

What happens

1. Microphone captures the sound wave.
2. It's split into frequency bands (a spectrum).
3. The averaged spectrum becomes a numeric fingerprint.
4. That fingerprint is stored under the animal's name.
In Live mode the system listens and matches new sounds to the closest fingerprint.
